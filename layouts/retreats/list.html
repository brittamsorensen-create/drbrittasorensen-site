The Experience

More than a cycling holiday

Cycling in the Mountains

Ride iconic Pyrenean routes in a small, supportive group. All abilities welcome — the focus is on experience and community, not pace.

Applied Sport Psychology

Evidence-based workshops and facilitated sessions covering mental performance, pain management, mindset, and resilience — practical tools you can use immediately.

Community & Connection

Small groups of women who share your passion for endurance sport and pushing limits. The connections formed here often last well beyond the retreat.
